Safety & privacy

When you need urgent help

If you are in danger, or thinking about harming yourself or others, please seek immediate help. Contact your local emergency number or a crisis hotline in your country. If you're in the U.S., you can call or text 988.

Privacy and data use

  • No sign-in required.
  • Messages are stored in your browser session and can be cleared or exported.
  • Chat messages are sent to the app backend to generate replies.
  • When AI replies are enabled, prompts may be forwarded to the Gemini API.
  • Voice features use your browser's speech services.

Stress detection (heuristics)

The assistant looks for common stress or anxiety phrases such as "panic", "can't breathe", and "overwhelmed". If risk-like language is detected, it will prioritize safety resources and supportive questions.

Conversation controls

Safety & privacy details

Non-clinical support

The assistant cannot provide diagnoses, prescribe medication, or guarantee outcomes. It aims to offer coping strategies, reflection, and suggestions for reaching professionals.

Mandatory safety behavior

If messages include crisis-like keywords such as "suicidal", "kill myself", "end my life", "hurt myself", "no reason to live", or explicit imminent threats, the assistant will:

  • Stop regular support responses and provide urgent help guidance.
  • Encourage contacting local emergency or crisis services or a trusted person.
  • Offer grounding steps while help is sought.

Privacy

This app sends chat messages to its backend and may forward prompts to the Gemini API to generate replies. Conversation history is stored in your browser session unless you export it. Microphone input and speech synthesis use browser-provided speech services, which may process audio depending on browser and network configuration.

Use responsibly

If you need medical help, contact a licensed clinician or local services.
