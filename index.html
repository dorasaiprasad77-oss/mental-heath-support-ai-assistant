Safety & Privacy Details

Non-clinical Support

The assistant cannot provide diagnoses, prescribe medication, or guarantee outcomes. It aims to offer coping strategies, reflection, and suggestions for reaching professionals.

Mandatory Safety Behavior

If messages include crisis-like keywords, the assistant will:

  • Stop “support-only” responses and provide urgent help guidance.
  • Encourage contacting local emergency/crisis services.
  • Offer grounding steps while help is sought.

Privacy

This page does not transmit your messages to a server. Microphone input and speech synthesis use your browser. Still, speech services may process audio depending on browser/network configuration.
